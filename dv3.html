Analysis

Very similarly to latitude vs humidity, there is not a clear relationship between latitude and cloudiness. The distribution is pretty equal across the board.

I am not really suprised that there isn't a clear connection between the two. I think cloudiness would probably have a stronger connection with something like proximity to mountains or large bodies of water. Our data set does not have this information so unfortunately we will not be able to do analysis on this. It may be interesting to look into at a later time.

Continuation

Though we don't see a relationship between latitude and cloudiness, we could also do a regression or p-test to show that there is no statistical relation between the two.
