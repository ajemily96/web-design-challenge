Data Visualization 1

Analysis

We can see that the maximum temperature for each city on 1/5/17 follows an arch pattern. This makes sense if we consider that the peak of that arch is right around 0 degrees (the equator), where we anticipate seeing the highest temperatures any time of year. We can also see that temperatures in the southern hemisphere are slightly higher than their positive latitude counterparts. This also makes sense to us because Janurary in the northern hemisphere is mid-winter.

Continuation

It would be interesting to visualize this data a different way. Maybe put some colors on each dot to indicate hemisphere. Or even break out the plot into two graphs (one for each hemisphere).

This would help the viewer see the pattern right off the bat. That's what visualizations are good for right? Helping people see patterns in their data.
